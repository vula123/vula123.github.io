Zofate zinga lengzem hla phuah thiam ber pawla chhal tel Lalzova Chhangte hi Hnuna leh Aimawii te inkarah kum 1924, Pawltlâk thla ni 2 khan Lunglei Rahsi vengah a lo piang a. A vanglai hun hi kum 1940-1945 inkar chauh nimah se, hla 48 vel a phuah hman a; hla phuahremtu(Songwriter) hlu tak a ni. Kum 21 mi chauh niin kum 1945, Pawltlâk thla ni 8 khan Khawsikpui natna avangin a thi ta a ni.

A hla phuah ṭhenkhatte chu -

1.Ka chantawk ka lungawi lo,

2.Par ang a chul,

3.Min ṭhen lul suh,

4.Ka rawn fang leh,

tihte hi a ni.
