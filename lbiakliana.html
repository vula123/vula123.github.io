L.Biakliana hi Saitual khuaah kum 1918, August ni 26 khan Tirhkoh Liangkhaia leh Ngurchhuani te nupa inkarah a lo piang a, a hun lai chuan Mizo zinga lehkha thiam ber pawl a ni. Kum 18 mi lek a nih laiin thawnthu ngaihnawm tak "Hawilopari" tih chu kum 1936 khan a ziak zo va, a kum lehah thawnthu tawi "Lali" tih a phuah leh a. Thawnthu ziak kawngah Zofate zingah kutchhuak tha pangngai nei hmasa a ni a, a kutchhuakte pawh hian belhchian a dawl hle.
