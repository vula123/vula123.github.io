Lalruali (1924-2001) hi kum 1924-ah Sihfaah a piang a, a pa chu Dokunga a ni. Naupang lehkha thiam thei tak a ni a, Pawl thum (Primary Scholarship) an exam kum dawn tak, 1936 aţangin Ruh Ngêt natna (Osteo-arthritis)-in a tlâkbuak ta a ni. Heti chung hian Pathianin Talent a pêk chu a hmang ţangkai hle a, hla 50 chuang a phuah a, lehkhabu ţha tak tak 3 a ziak bawk a ni. Presbyterian Hospital, Durtlanga Evangelist hna kum 35 a thawh hnu-in kum 1997 aţangin a chawlhsan a, kum 1999 aţangin Durtlang Leitan South-a Upa Vanchhunga (a laizawn fapa) te inah a chêng a, kum 2001, mimkut thla ni 3 khan a boral a, Durtlang Leitan Thlanmualah phum a ni ta a ni. Lalruali hla phuah hmasak ber chu “Ka Chenna ram thlaler a ni Lalpa” tih, kum 1958-a a phuah hi a ni.

A hla phuahte-

1.Ka chenna ram thlaler a ni Lalpa (kum 1958)

2.Hneh theih loh nun ka nei (kum 1965, ramtuk thla; Thlûk : Rokunga)

tihte hi a ni.
